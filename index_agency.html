For Government Agencies, Charities and Merchants

How it works

Government and charity staff are equipped with an admin portal to perform tasks needed to manage a voucher campaign.

Digitize vouchers in minutes

Agencies can create and send digital vouchers via SMS to recipients for them to redeem goods and services at any participating merchant. No app download needed, just send and go.

Prevents vouchers from being used more than once

Merchants can connect with the RedeemSG system via APIs to check the validity of a voucher and update that a voucher was used at checkout. Upon redemption update, the voucher is refreshed, preventing it from being used again.

Live voucher status update

Redeem dashboard updates in real time on whether a voucher is sent, seen, redeemed or whether refunds are processed.

Statistics help organizers optimise campaign

Dashboard provides statistics such as how many vouchers are redeemed and what proportion of vouchers are redeemed so far.

Faster Reconciliation

Transaction reports allow government agencies to reconcile and settle with merchants easily.

Manage multiple campaigns at once

Portal allows agencies and charities to oversee and update multiple campaigns.
