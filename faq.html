About RedeemSG

RedeemSG is a voucher system that helps Singapore Government agencies easily create, issue and track the redemption of vouchers. It also enables merchants to easily accept Government issued vouchers. This comprises of:

  • An admin portal that allows agencies to create vouchers, send vouchers and track voucher redemptions.
  • Singpass sign-up for vouchers for beneficiaries.
  • E-vouchers that get sent to citizens via SMS that open up in a web link, and
  • Easy merchant onboarding via the RedeemSG Merchant app or integration with an existing POS system using an API.

Any government agency and its affiliate partners can use RedeemSG to create, send and track vouchers. We also assess requests from charities on a case by case basis.

Government officers can log into RedeemSG using their gov.sg email, whereas charities would need to contact us to gain access to the portal. To create and gain access to a campaign, or to gain access to the portal, please contact us at feedback@redeem.gov.sg.

Beneficiaries of a campaign run by the Government agency/ charity will receive an SMS sent by RedeemSG that opens up a RedeemSG e-voucher link in the browser.

  • Government agencies provide us with their campaign specifications to create a campaign.
  • Agencies send sign-up link to recipients to claim their vouchers using Singpass.
  • Recipients sign up using Singpass and receive an SMS with their unique voucher link.
  • Recipients open the voucher in a web link and show the voucher QR code to the merchant, who will scan using a barcode scanner or mobile app.

With merchants scanning the customers’ QR code, the same workflow can be used by both merchants and both digitally and less digitally savvy customers. The flow involves the customer presenting a voucher QR (both digital or physical) and merchants scanning.

RedeemSG has explored multiple digital solutions including a Government Wallet system. While this option is technically workable, it requires all citizens to download a mobile app to be able to use their vouchers. This would exclude the less digitally savvy citizens who are unable to download an app.

To ensure that the merchant app is easy to use, we have run a series of 8 trials with heartland merchants and hawkers since March 2020. The solution is also used for various Government voucher campaigns.

For Merchants

RedeemSG allows merchants to easily accept Government-issued vouchers using the RedeemSG Merchant app. The key benefits are:

  • No commissions or fees
  • Fuss-free redemption and transaction tracking
  • No handling of physical vouchers

Upon registration of interest, a staff would be in touch with you to conduct the onboarding session if you are eligible. During the session, the staff will provide you with the 6-character shop code you require to log in. Separately, an SMS will also be sent to you with the unique shop code.

Yes! Your staff can download the RedeemSG Merchant app using their own device, and log-in using the same shop code that was issued to you via SMS. They will be able to scan vouchers, and view transactions and payouts, but will not be able to edit any merchant details such as address or bank details.
