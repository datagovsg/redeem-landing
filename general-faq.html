About RedeemSG

RedeemSG is a solution being used to enable government agencies and charities to easily track redemption of vouchers. This comprises of:

  • An admin portal that allows agencies to create vouchers, send vouchers and track voucher redemptions.
  • E-vouchers that get sent to citizens via SMS that open up in a web link, and
  • an API that merchants can integrate with to instantly invalidate vouchers.

Any government agency and its affiliate partners can use RedeemSG. We also assess requests from charities on a case by case basis.

To gain access to the system, you will need to contact us at feedback@redeem.gov.sg to set up a campaign.

Updating Redemption of Goods

Each person can only redeem their quota for a particular campaign (E.g. 1 mask pack, or 4 meals a day). Any additional redemption requests should be declined. If a person insists they did not redeem but it is shown in the system that they redeemed, you can still click “redeem anyway” and log this as an appeal case.

For campaigns involving distributions to individuals, the NRIC/FIN will typically be used as the form of identification.

RedeemSG app enables users to scan any Singapore identification card that includes the NRIC or FIN number. This includes the NRIC card, student card, passion card, birth certificate and various foreign passes issued by MOM (e.g. work pass, S pass, entrepass, long term visit pass).

For campaigns involving distribution to households, the postal code, floor and unit number will be used to identify a household. Any valid Singapore address can be logged by the system.

Please check with the campaign owner for the forms of identification that a person would need to bring down to redeem an item for a particular campaign, as this is determined by them.

Adding and Changing Collection Points

Please find your collection point’s Point-of-Contact (POC) and scan their QR code to add a collection point.

Alternatively, have them send the QR code over whatsapp to your fellow volunteers/ staff or print the QR code out for scanning. These QR codes change each day, so you will need to scan the QR code every day to update redemption.

QR codes are valid each day till 11.59pm, and reset and midnight. This is to ensure that users who only help out only on certain days of the campaign will not be able to update redemption for the other days.
