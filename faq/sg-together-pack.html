About RedeemSG App

RedeemSG is a solution being used to enable government agencies and affiliate organizations to easily track redemption of goods and vouchers. This comprises of:

  • A mobile app used by agency officers and merchants to update redemption,
  • An admin portal that allows agencies to manage their campaign and view statistics, and
  • API that merchants can integrate with to update redemption.

Any government agency and its affiliate partners can use RedeemSG. We also assess requests from charities on a case by case basis.

To gain access to the system, you will need to contact us at feedback@redeem.gov.sg to set up a campaign.

  • We will need to add mobile numbers of Campaign administrators and operations personnel before you and your colleagues can start using the mobile app for a particular campaign.
  • We will add your mobile numbers such that you will be able to access a campaign on the admin portal.

You will need to either be added to a campaign as a campaign/ location administrator or scan the QR code of a collection point before you can start using RedeemSG to update redemption for a particular campaign.

You will need to be added to a campaign as a campaign administrator before you can start using RedeemSG to update redemptions for recipients.

Adding and Changing Collection Points

Please find your collection point’s Point-of-Contact (POC) and scan their QR code to add a collection point.

Alternatively, have them send the QR code over whatsapp to your fellow volunteers/ staff or print the QR code out for scanning. These QR codes change each day, so you will need to scan the QR code every day to update redemption.

QR codes are valid each day till 11.59pm, and reset and midnight. This is to ensure that users who only help out only on certain days of the campaign will not be able to update redemption for the other days.

Watch this video from 4.33 onwards. Otherwise, the instructions are below as well.

  • Click on the menu button on the top left hand corner that looks like 3 lines
  • Click on your current location, e.g. “Jurong Green CC”
  • Find the new collection point’s location POC, and scan the QR code of your new collection point. You can skip this step if all you want to do is to switch collection point.
  • Switch collection point. Save changes.

Distributing SG Together Pack and Singapore Flag

Recipients will need to bring down their NRIC to collect the SG Together Pack. Singpass version of NRIC is also accepted. Photocopies and screenshots will not be accepted.

You will need to key in the address reflected on the person’s NRIC. If people’s address is different from that which is reflected on the NRIC, they are advised to visit any nearby Neighbourhood Police Centre/ Post or the Citizen Services Centre with NRIC and proof of residential address to update their address. More details can be found at https://www.gov.sg/article/changing-your-address.

Otherwise, they can bring along a letter sent by a government agency such as from IRAS, ICA, LTA or recent bills from Town Council or Singapore Power along with your NRIC for collection.

No, NRIC must be presented to collect the Singapore Together Pack.

For addresses without a floor and unit number, you will need to tick the check box if floor and unit number is not applicable to the address.

For addresses with floor but no unit number, you can key in the floor, but leave the unit number blank.

For addresses with unit number but no floor number, you can key in the unit number and leave the floor blank.

The general principle is that you will need to reference and key in the person’s address as reflected on NRIC or letter. There is an option in the app to “redeem anyway” for such cases.

Some people have NRICs with addresses that are based on the 4 digit postal code format. You will need to key in the 4 digit postal code into the app and click “use this postal code”.

You can do so by ticking “opt in” on the mobile app.

For appeal cases, you will need to tick the “opt in checkbox here.

People are highly encouraged to collect their Singapore Together Pack from their designated CCs or RCs Centres during the given collection windows. They may find this information on the sgtogetherpack.gowhere.gov.sg website by keying in their postal code.

However, the app does not restrict people from collecting at the designated CCs or RCs.
