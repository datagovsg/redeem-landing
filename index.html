RedeemSG is used by
Singapore government agencies to easily create, send and
track redemption of e-vouchers.

Digitizes vouchers in minutes. No more manual tracking of redemptions.

For Voucher Recipients

How it works

Receive a SMS with a voucher link.

Click on the voucher link to view voucher, present it to participating merchants at checkout.

For more info on the programme, visit the link at the bottom of the voucher or email the agency contact. You can also find more info on the specific programme at our FAQs

For Government Agencies, Charities and Merchants

How it works

Agencies can see the voucher status and redemption statistics updating live on the dashboard

Reach almost
everyone

Agencies can create and send digital vouchers via SMS to recipients for them to redeem goods and services at any participating merchant. No app download needed, just send and go.

Invalidates voucher in real time

Merchants can connect with Redeem to check the validity of a voucher and update that a voucher was used at checkout. Prevents double redemption

Faster Reconciliation

Transaction reports allow government agencies to reconcile and settle with merchants easily.
