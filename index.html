The trusted voucher system for the Singapore Government

RedeemSG is a voucher system that helps Singapore Government agencies easily create, issue and track the redemption of vouchers. It also enables merchants to easily accept Government issued vouchers.
See all available vouchers

Recipients

Recipients can sign up and claim a voucher without needing to download a mobile app.

Merchants

Merchants can accept vouchers easily using the RedeemSG Merchant App.

Government Agencies

Agencies can manage voucher campaign(s) and track redemptions using the RedeemSG Admin Portal.

For Recipients

Claim your voucher

Visit the voucher campaign sign up page to claim your voucher.

Receive a voucher link sent via SMS

Only trust voucher links from
  • voucher.redeem.gov.sg
  • v.redeem.gov.sg

Show QR code to participating merchants to spend your voucher

Spend your voucher at participating merchants. Participating merchants will be provided by the campaign organiser.

For more info on the programme, visit the link at the bottom of the voucher or email the agency contact. You can also find more info on the specific programme in the FAQ page

For Merchants (CDC Vouchers Scheme)

Indicate your interest & get registered

Indicate your interest on this form. Sign up with a Digital Ambassador or CDC Ambassador. Receive a shop code.

Download the app

Download the RedeemSG Merchant app on the App Store or Google Play.

Onboard your shop onto RedeemSG

Key in the shop code to gain access to your shop account. Other staff can also onboard using this same shop code.

For more info on the programme, visit the link at the bottom of the voucher or email the agency contact. You can also find more info on the specific programme in the FAQ page
